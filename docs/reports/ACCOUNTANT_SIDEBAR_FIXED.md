## 🎯 What was fixed:

### 1. **Sidebar Visibility for Accountant**
I realized I had previously hidden "Sales" and "People" sections for the Accountant role. This was incorrect as accountants need to validate invoices and check customer/supplier ledgers.

**Changes Made:**
- ✅ **Sales & Billing**: Restored visibility. Accountants can now see Invoices/Quotations.
- ✅ **People**: Restored visibility. Accountants can now see Customers/Suppliers.
- ✅ **Reports**: Explicitly enabled.
- ✅ **Finance**: Explicitly enabled.
- ❌ **Stock/Products**: Kept hidden (irrelevant for daily accounting).

### 2. **Final Sidebar Structure for Accountant**
When logged in as an Accountant, the sidebar will now show:
1.  **Dashboard** (Accountant Dashboard)
2.  **Sales & Billing** (Invoices, Quotations)
3.  **Finance** (Expenses)
4.  **People** (Customers, Suppliers)
5.  **Analytics** (Reports)

This is the standard, perfect sidebar for an accounting role in StockSathi.
